Coffee Classics & Variations

From bold and intense to creamy and sweet, coffee offers a huge range of flavor profiles.

Spanish Latte

A creamy, sweetened latte made with condensed milk. It’s smoother and richer than a standard latte.

Sea Salt Latte

Usually a cold brew or iced Americano topped with a thick layer of salted cream foam. It strikes a perfect balance between savory and sweet.

Caramel Macchiato

A layered drink featuring vanilla syrup, steamed milk, espresso, and a generous drizzle of caramel sauce.

Dirty Chai Latte

A fusion drink consisting of a spiced chai tea latte with a shot of espresso added for an extra caffeine boost.

Cold Brew with Oat Milk

Steeped for 12–24 hours, this coffee is low in acidity and pairs beautifully with the nuttiness of oat milk.

Matcha Selections

Matcha provides a steady energy release without the jittery feeling often associated with coffee.

Iced Strawberry Matcha Latte

A trendy favorite featuring a layer of fresh strawberry purée, milk, and a vibrant green matcha topper.

Ceremonial Grade Hot Matcha

Prepared whisked with hot water (not boiling) to enjoy the pure, grassy, and umami notes of high-quality tea.

Vanilla Matcha Latte

A great entry-level matcha; the vanilla helps mellow out the natural earthiness of the green tea.

Coconut Matcha Refresher

Matcha mixed with coconut water or coconut milk for a light, tropical, and hydrating twist.

Matcha Espresso Fusion

For those who can't decide—this drink layers matcha, milk, and a shot of espresso for a beautiful tri-color effect.
